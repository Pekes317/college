About Us

Larry Schneider, owner of Homestyle Custom Upholstery, started doing upholstery in 1973. After attending MATC Upholstery School, he then worked for a few shops in the Milwaukee area. Making the decision to open his own business in 1983, the first location was a 3500 square foot building on 27th Street and Ryan Road in Oak Creek Wisconsin. Being very happy there, but due to expansion of the business,relocation was necessary. The move to 9825 S. 13th Street in Oak Creek Wisconsin was made in June of 2005.

With four full time employees, it's a bustling place!

Craig Wartner, who has over twenty years experience in the awning trade.

Patty Jaworsky who worked twelve years in the awning industry.

Bruce Wilcox has been in the carpet installation business for fifteen years and with us for six years trained in carpet, convertible tops, vinyl tops, and all types of upholstery.

Eric Konster came to us via upper Michigan where he attended Northern Michigan University and DCE Vocational School and was certified for sewing and upholstery. He now attends MATC Milwaukee. He worked at Second Nature Canvas and Upholstery in Marquette Michigan.

Eric started with us in 2005 and is a fast learner, Industrious, and a trustworthy worker.

When the shop was opened in 1983, it was just Larry and one helper, who still comes by and helps out in a cinch, if we get backlogged or have a rush job. In the busy summer hours there are a few part time employees to help out.
